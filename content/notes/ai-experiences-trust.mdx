---
title: "Why AI experiences need trust."
---

Generative AI is powerful and unreliable in the same breath. Users know this. 

The worst AI products assume competence and skip explanation. They drop a generated paragraph on the screen as if it were absolute truth.

The best ones show their work. They acknowledge their limits. They provide citations. They give users a way back when the system inevitably hallucinates. Trust is the only infrastructure that matters in AI UX. If you lose it, the user will revert to their manual, tedious workflow immediately—not because it's better, but because it's predictable.
