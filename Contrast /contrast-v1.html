How it works

From URL to shareable report in four steps.

01
Paste your URL

Enter any publicly accessible website. No login, no extension, no setup needed.

https://yoursite.com
02
We run the audit

A headless browser loads the page and runs WCAG checks across contrast, images, type, and layout.

~10 seconds
03
Read your report

Get a scored report with every issue listed by severity, CSS selector, and measured value.

Scored 0–100
04
Share or download

Every audit gets a permanent URL. Share with your team or download a one-page PDF.

PDF · Link · OG card

What we check

Four categories, weighted by impact on real users.

◑

Color contrast

Every text–background pair measured against WCAG 2.1 AA. We surface the actual ratio so you know exactly what needs fixing.

Threshold: 4.5:1 normal · 3:1 large text
Weight: 40% of overall score
⊡

Alt text coverage

Every meaningful image checked for a non-empty alt attribute. Decorative images with role="presentation" correctly excluded.

WCAG 1.1.1 — Level A
Weight: 30% of overall score
Aa

Typography consistency

Counts distinct font families loaded on the page. More than two almost always indicates design system inconsistency.

Target: ≤2 font families
Weight: 15% of overall score
⊞

Spacing consistency

Margin and padding values sampled and checked against an 8px grid. Off-grid values signal ad-hoc spacing that breaks visual rhythm.

Grid: 8px base unit
Weight: 15% of overall score

Error states

Calm and reassuring. Every error gives a clear next step.

ERR_INVALID_URL
⌗

That doesn't look like a valid URL

Try including https:// at the start. We need a full address to reach the page and run an audit.

Try again →
ERR_UNREACHABLE
⚡

We couldn't reach that page

The page didn't respond within 15 seconds. It might be down or blocking automated browsers. Try a different URL.

Try again →
ERR_LOGIN_REQUIRED
🔒

This page requires a login

Contrast only audits publicly accessible pages. Try the homepage or a public marketing page instead.

Audit homepage instead →
ERR_TIMEOUT
⏱

This page is taking too long

The page took over 15 seconds to load. It may have heavy scripts or a slow server. Try again or use a lighter URL.

Retry →
ERR_RATE_LIMITED
⏸

You've hit the hourly limit

10 audits per hour keeps the tool free for everyone. Your limit resets at 12:32 PM — about 28 minutes from now.

View past audits →
ERR_UNKNOWN
◌

Something went wrong

Your URL has been saved. Give it a moment — this usually clears up on its own. If it keeps happening, try a different page.

Retry →
